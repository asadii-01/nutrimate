# NutriMate: Smart Nutrition for Real Life
## Target Audience
- University students in Pakistan (hostel-friendly meals).
- Gym beginners (macro tracking, goal setting).
- Budget-conscious users (low-cost meal options).

## Visual Identity
- Theme: Light, airy, healthy.
- Primary Color: #16A34A (Leaf Green).
- Secondary: #F97316 (Calorie Orange), #3B82F6 (Hydration Blue).
- Corner Radius: 16px.

## Screen List
1. Landing Page (Public)
2. Login & Register (Split layout)
3. Profile Setup (4-step wizard)
4. Dashboard (Personalized home)
5. Meal Recommendations (Browse & Swap)
6. Nutrition Search (Food database)
7. Progress Analytics (Charts & Insights)
8. Profile / Settings (User management)
9. States (Empty, Loading, Error)

## Reusable Components
- Top Navbar & Bottom Tab Bar
- Meal Card (kcal, macros, hostel-friendly tags)
- Progress Rings (calories, water)
- Stat Cards with deltas
- Detailed Drawers/Modals
